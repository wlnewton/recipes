Mediterranean Orzo

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Orzo
  • Feta Cheese
  • Cucumber
  • Red Onion
  • 1/4 cup Parsley
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper

optional

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Cook chicken if using.
  2. Cook Orzo according to directions.
  3. Cut cucumber, tomato, and onion to desired size.
  4. In a small bowl, mix oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  5. In a large bowl, mix together the orzo and oil mixture first, then stir in vegetables, cheese, and chicken.
